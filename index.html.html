About My School

Presbyterian Boys' Secondary School is the best all-boys boarding school located in Legon, Accra, Ghana. It was established in 1938 by the Presbyterian Church of the Gold Coast, PRESEC is known as one of Ghana’s top secondary schools, known for its excellence in academics, leadership, and moral discipline. The school’s motto, In Lumine Tuo Videbimus Lumen ("In Thy Light, We Shall See Light"), reflects its Christian foundation and values. Its students, known as "PRESECANS," are celebrated for their academic and extracurricular achievements, making PRESEC a respected name across Ghana and beyond.

Mission

The mission of Presbyterian Boys' Secondary School (PRESEC) is to provide holistic education that balances academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills. Rooted in Christian values, the school aims to nurture disciplined, confident, and morally responsible young men who will excel in their chosen fields and contribute positively to society. PRESEC achieves this by fostering a supportive and rigorous learning environment that encourages intellectual growth, ethical integrity, and service to God and country.

Vision

The vision of Presbyterian Boys' Secondary School (PRESEC) is to be a leading center of excellence in secondary education in Ghana, producing well-rounded young men who are not only academically outstanding but also morally upright and equipped with the skills needed to excel in a dynamic, global society. The school aspires to develop future leaders who embody Christian values, integrity, and a commitment to making meaningful contributions to their communities and the world.
